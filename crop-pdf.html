What is PDF Cropping?

PDF cropping is the process of removing unwanted outer areas from PDF pages. This online PDF cropper allows you to trim margins, cut out unnecessary sections, or focus on specific content within your documents. Whether you need to remove white space, crop scanned documents, or extract specific diagrams, our tool makes it simple.

Unlike traditional PDF editors that require software installation, our browser-based PDF cropper works entirely on your device. Your files remain private and secure as they never leave your computer. You can crop multiple pages at once with the same selection or customize each page individually.

The resulting PDF maintains the quality of your original content while giving you a cleaner, more focused document. This is particularly useful for presentations, academic papers, or preparing documents for specific formatting requirements.

Why Crop a PDF?

  • Remove unwanted margins: Eliminate excess white space or borders around your content.
  • Focus on important content: Crop out irrelevant sections, ads, or annotations from scanned documents.
  • Prepare for printing: Adjust page dimensions to fit specific paper sizes or printing requirements.
  • Extract specific elements: Isolate diagrams, charts, or images from larger pages.
  • Reduce file size: Cropping removes unnecessary areas, which can reduce overall file size.
  • Professional presentation: Create cleaner documents by removing distracting elements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my PDF uploaded to your servers when I crop it?

No, all cropping happens locally in your browser. Your PDF files never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Can I crop multiple pages in a PDF?

Yes, you can crop all pages at once with the same crop area, or crop each page individually with custom selections.

What PDF versions are supported?

Our PDF cropper works with most PDF versions. The tool renders pages as images for cropping, so the resulting PDF will be image-based.

Is there a limit on PDF file size or number of pages?

Since processing happens in your browser, the limit depends on your device's memory and performance. Larger files with many pages may take longer to process.

Can I undo a crop after applying it?

Yes, you can adjust the crop area before downloading. If you need to start over, you can re-upload the original PDF.
