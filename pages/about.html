About PDF99

Simple, fast, and privacy-focused PDF utilities that run in your browser - no installs, minimal tracking.

Privacy-first

What we do

PDF99 offers a compact collection of PDF tools - merge, split, convert between PDF and Office formats, compress, and basic OCR. Our goal is to make common document tasks reliable and available directly in the browser with an emphasis on privacy and performance.

Design principles

  • Local-first: run work in the browser where possible so files never leave the user’s device.
  • Minimalism: focused tools that do one thing well and load quickly.
  • Privacy by default: limited telemetry, temporary server processing only when needed, and transparent retention policies.
  • Accessibility: clean markup, keyboard support, and ARIA where appropriate.

Our story

PDF99 began as a small side project to solve the recurring problem of quick PDF edits without installing heavy software. Over time it evolved into a lightweight toolset used by individuals and teams who value speed and privacy.

Open to collaboration

We welcome feedback, contributions, and partnerships. If you’d like to integrate PDF99 into a workflow, suggest a feature, or report an issue, reach out via Contact or email support@pdf99.org.
