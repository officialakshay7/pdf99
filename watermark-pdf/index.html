What is a PDF watermark?

A watermark is a semi-transparent text or image overlaid on document pages to indicate status, ownership or handling instructions. Common watermarks include CONFIDENTIAL, DRAFT, DO NOT COPY or a company name. They signal to recipients how a document should be treated without obscuring the content. PDF watermarks are added to the page canvas rather than embedded in the page text, so they appear on every page when viewed or printed.
