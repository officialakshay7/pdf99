What is a PDF Converter?

A PDF converter is a tool that transforms documents and images into PDF format. Whether you need to create a print-ready file, preserve formatting, or share images as a single document, PDF converters make the process fast and reliable. PDF99 supports image to PDF, JPG to PDF, Word to PDF, and more.

Why Convert Images to PDF?

  • Universal access: PDFs open the same way across devices and platforms.
  • Organize easily: Merge multiple images into a single PDF for better management.
  • Smaller size: Compress images into lightweight PDFs suitable for email and storage.
  • Professional format: PDFs preserve layout and make sharing polished and secure.

Image to PDF - Complete Guide

Converting images such as JPG, PNG, or WebP to PDF is common when you need to share, archive, or print files. A PDF keeps everything consistent and easy to manage. With PDF99, you can merge multiple images into one document, adjust page size, and export quickly without losing quality.

Best practices

  1. Pick the right page size: A4 for international, Letter for US printing.
  2. Check orientation: Rotate your images before conversion for clean results.
  3. Use compression: For large image sets, compress PDF files to reduce space.
  4. Preserve transparency: PNGs converted to PDF keep their transparency intact.

When to use

Use PDF conversion for scanned receipts, photos, ID proofs, reports, and designs. It’s especially useful when emailing or submitting documents online, since PDFs are universally accepted and easy to read.
